Lelit Espresso Machine Issues

Authorized Lelit service provider — troubleshooting guide for all Lelit machines

  • Low or No Espresso Pressure

    Common causes: Worn group head gasket, blocked shower screen, scale buildup in boiler, failing pump, or incorrect grind size.

    Diagnosis steps:

    • Check the pressure gauge during extraction (should read 9 bar)
    • Remove and inspect the shower screen for scale or coffee buildup
    • Replace the group head gasket if it's cracked or hardened (recommended annually)
    • Run a full descale cycle — scale buildup severely restricts flow
    • If pump is making unusual noise, it may be failing
    • Adjust grind coarser if the shot is pulling too slowly (< 20 seconds)

  • Temperature Management: Single Boiler vs. HX Machines

    Lelit Anita (single boiler with PID): The Anita uses a 250ml brass boiler with PID temperature control. Because it's a single boiler, it uses the same boiler for brewing and steaming — so you'll need to wait for the temperature to drop back down to brew temp after steaming. The PID makes this easier to manage than older single-boiler machines.

    • Allow the machine to reach full brew temperature before pulling shots (watch the PID display)
    • After steaming, wait for the boiler to cool back to brew temperature before the next shot
    • The 3-way valve on the Anita will dry out the puck after each shot — this is normal
    • If temperature is inconsistent despite stable PID readings, the boiler may need service

    Lelit Mara X, Elizabeth, Bianca (HX/dual boiler machines): These machines require a cooling flush before pulling espresso shots.

    • After the machine heats up, run 2–3 oz of water through the group before your shot
    • After steaming milk, always do a longer cooling flush (4–5 oz)
    • If shots taste sour, the brew water is too cool — reduce flush amount
    • If shots taste bitter, brew water is too hot — increase flush amount
    • If temperature swings are extreme, the thermosiphon circuit may be blocked — professional service needed

  • Steam Wand Problems

    No steam or weak steam:

    • Blocked steam tip holes — remove tip and soak in hot water or clear with a pin
    • Steam boiler scale — descale immediately if you haven't done so in 3+ months
    • Steam solenoid valve issue — may need replacement

    Steam wand won't close (continuous drip):

    • Steam valve O-ring worn — replace the steam knob valve assembly
    • Steam tip partially blocked causing back pressure
    • On Lelit Bianca: check the steam paddle valve for wear

  • Water Leaks

    Leak from group head:

    • Group gasket needs replacement (most common — replace annually)
    • Shower screen screw loose
    • E61 group head mushroom valve stuck open (Mara X, Bianca, Elizabeth — not applicable to Anita, Anna, or Fred which use Lelit's own group head)

    Leak from boiler or fittings:

    • Stop using the machine immediately to prevent electrical damage
    • Boiler fitting threads may have corroded — professional repair needed
    • Expansion valve (OPV) dripping is normal during heating — abnormal after warmup

    Leak from water reservoir area:

    • Reservoir O-ring worn or improperly seated
    • Reservoir cracked — requires replacement

  • Weak steam
  • Water leaks anywhere
  • Loud pump noise
  • E61 group dripping
